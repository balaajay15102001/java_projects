Overview

Rathinam School of Architecture (RSA) is an institution established by an elite panel of practitioners and academicians with an intention to redefine learning and set new standards in Architectural education, design, research, and scholarship to create an engagement between academia, research, and industry.

Listed as one of the best architecture colleges in Coimbatore, RSA constantly explores the domain of Design & Architecture through intense architectural dialogue, inquiry, experimentation, and continued research to evolve industry-ready professionals with aptitude necessary to emerge as leaders and critical thinkers in the field of architecture.
